What is Physiotherapy?

Your Body Has the Power to Heal — We Help It.

Physiotherapy is a healthcare treatment that helps you restore movement, reduce pain and strengthen your body — without relying only on medicines or surgery.

Think of it as expert, hands-on care for your muscles, joints, nerves and bones. Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing arthritis, or dealing with everyday stiffness — physiotherapy gives your body the tools to heal naturally.

No Surgery, No Side Effects Natural, evidence-based treatments that work with your body.
Personalised Treatment Plans Every patient is different — your care plan is built just for you.
Long-Term Relief We fix the root cause, not just mask the pain.

What We Treat

Conditions We Specialise In

From everyday aches to complex conditions — expert physiotherapy for every need.

🔙

Back Pain

Relief for lower and upper back pain from posture, muscle strain, disc issues or daily wear and tear.

🦒

Neck Pain

Treatment for stiff neck, cervical spondylosis, and pain from long hours on screens or poor posture.

🦵

Knee Pain

Effective care for knee joint pain, ligament issues, cartilage wear and pain while walking or climbing stairs.

👟

Heel Pain

Treatment for plantar fasciitis and heel spurs — common cause of sharp morning foot pain.

🦴

Arthritis

Gentle physiotherapy to reduce joint stiffness, swelling and pain — keeping you active and comfortable.

⚡

Sciatica Pain

Targeted treatment for the shooting pain that travels from your lower back down the leg.

😶

Bell's Palsy

Specialised facial nerve rehabilitation to restore movement and muscle tone.

💪

Frozen Shoulder

Step-by-step rehabilitation to restore shoulder range of motion and eliminate adhesive capsulitis pain.

⚽

Sports Injuries

Recovery and prevention for athletes — sprains, strains, muscle tears and overuse injuries.

🧘

Soreness & Relaxation

Therapeutic massage and manual techniques to ease muscle soreness, tension and body fatigue.

🪡

Dry Needling

Fine needles release muscle knots (trigger points), reduce pain and improve blood flow in tight muscles.

🫙

Cupping Therapy

Suction cups improve circulation, relieve tension and speed up recovery — proven ancient technique.

🫂

Paralysis Rehabilitation

Compassionate, structured physiotherapy for partial or complete paralysis — restoring mobility, strength and independence through neuro-rehabilitation.
